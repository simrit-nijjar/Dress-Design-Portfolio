Our Story

My journey to this point began when I took an interest in creating and sewing at a young age. A spark was ignited which lead to a burning desire to create and design. My passion grew every year until I finally decided to take the first major step and learn how to professionally match and design fabrics. This first step trickled down to many years of experience building with a small client base until I finally decided to start Punjabi Designer Boutique.
